How to Play Greed Dice

Goal: Be the first player to reach the target score.

On your turn:

  • Roll the dice to add points to your current score.
  • If you roll a 1, you lose your current score and your turn ends.
  • Press Hold to bank your current score and pass the turn.

Controls:

  • Space or D — Roll dice
  • Enter or H — Hold
  • R — New game
  • Escape — Close this modal

Click a player's name to rename them. Choose your target score above.
